Importer of Records

The Importer of Record (IOR) service is a customs and logistics service that helps businesses import goods into a country. The IOR is responsible for ensuring that all customs documentation is properly completed, taxes and duties are paid, and the goods are cleared for entry into the country.

Our IOR service includes:

• Identifying and obtaining the necessary import licenses and permits

• Completing and submitting customs declarations

• Paying customs duties and taxes on behalf of the importing business

• Providing advice on customs regulations and procedures

• Coordinating the transportation and delivery of the goods

In addition to providing these services, we also act as the legal representative of the importing business, taking on any legal responsibility and liability for the imported goods. This can provide peace of mind to businesses and help ensure smooth and compliant imports.

Overall, our service helps businesses navigate the complex and often confusing world of international trade, allowing them to easily and efficiently import goods into a country.
